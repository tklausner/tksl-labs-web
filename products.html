Our Products

Gonorrhea is a very common disease impacting over 85,000,000 people a year. Unteated gonorrhea can cause complications in men and women including chronic pain and infertilty. In pregnant women untreated gonorrhea can cause premature birth and neonatal blindess. Unfortunately, gonorrhea is becoming increasingly resistant to common antibiotics and harder to treat. The US CDC currently recommends gonorrhea treatment with a single injection of ceftriaxone In Asia, up to 10% of cases of gonorrhea may be resistant to ceftriaxone. Resistant cases have also been reported in the United Kingdom and Australia.

ResistancePredict uses our novel technology to prectict resistance to ceftriaxone and similar class antibiotics with > 99% accuracy.
